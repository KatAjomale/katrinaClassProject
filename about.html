What is Reflexology?

Reflexology is an ancient therapy first used in Egypt in 2,330 BC. It is a therapy that is based on the principle that there are reflex areas in the feet and hands that correspond to all of the parts of the body. It helps to relieve tension; it helps improve blood supply to all parts of the body; it helps rid the body of toxins; and by doing all of this it helps the body achieve balance so it can then try to heal itself.

About the Reflexologist

Sue Chappell is a member of the Association of Reflexologists, (AoR) having completed a years course accredited by the association. All members of the AoR, by joining the association, undertake to abide by the Code of Professional Practice and Ethics.

The Code of Practice and Ethics is designed to encourage integrity and responsibility in the practice of Reflexology and to uphold and further the standing of the profession and the AoR.
